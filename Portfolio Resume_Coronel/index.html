About Me

I'm Mark Oliver Coronel a UI/UX designer, developer, and creative thinker based in Manguisoc, Mercedes, Daet Camarines Norte. I craft digital experiences that blend minimalist aesthetics with functional design.

With a passion for clean interfaces and thoughtful interactions, I transform ideas into engaging, user-centered web experiences. My work combines creativity, precision, and purpose to bring modern design to life.

Beyond coding and design, I’m driven by curiosity — exploring new technologies, experimenting with visual styles, and finding inspiration in art, fashion, and architecture.

My Gallery

Latest Work

Fitpulse

FitPulse is a health and wellness web application designed to help users monitor and improve their daily lifestyle. The platform tracks key health aspects such as sleep, exercise, and nutrition, providing personalized insights for maintaining a balanced and fit body.

Healthier You

Healthier You is a modern web platform designed for licensed nutritionists and dietitians to streamline their client booking process. The website provides an intuitive interface where clients can easily schedule consultations, access nutrition programs, and connect directly with professionals.

iTour Mercedes

iTourMercedes is an information and booking website designed to promote tourism in Mercedes, Camarines Norte. The platform allows tourists to explore island destinations, view travel details, and conveniently book or inquire online.
